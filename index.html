COVID Protocol

At the reunion we have are going to have plenty of children too young to get vaccinated and beloved elders who are a more at risk population. We ask that you help make sure every one can get to Fisher Reunion 2025 by taking the following precautions:

  • During this weekend we will be doing as many activities outside as possible.
  • Please try to limit unnecessary exposure in the week leading up to the reunion
  • We strongly encourage testing before attending, doubly so if you are feeling any symptoms. If you test positive, we ask you sit this one out.
  • If you have home tests, please bring some along
  • Please bring several masks along. They are not required, but it is good to have them on hand
  • If you start to have symptoms, please take one of the home tests. We will have a separate quarantine space for you if you are positive

Bring any snacks if you would like! We are putting a lot of thought into making our snack table more covid conscious, so take that into consideration when choosing what snacks to bring.
Have a kid you need to entertain? Be on the lookout for a kids table set up with activities for the young (or young at heart!) to do.
Bring some games to play with others!
